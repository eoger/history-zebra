Insert a comma separated list of domains.

All subdomains will be included:

  • google.com will include mail.google.com and www.google.com.
  • mail.yahoo.com will include us.mail.yahoo.com but not yahoo.com.
 

In whitelist mode, only these domains will be allowed in your browser history.
In blacklist mode, they are the ones which will be forbidden.
In either mode, if no domains are specified, all browser history will be kept.
